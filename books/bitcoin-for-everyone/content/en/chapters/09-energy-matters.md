# Why Energy Matters

## Chapter Digest

Energy is not an awkward side effect of Bitcoin. It is part of the monetary logic that gives the system credibility. A money that cannot be cheaply faked needs a real-world cost, and Bitcoin secures itself through proof of work, where energy expenditure helps make the network hard to corrupt, hard to counterfeit, and hard to dominate by fiat.

That changes the question from whether Bitcoin uses energy to what that energy secures. If the expenditure is what protects scarcity, ordering, and neutrality, then it is not waste in the abstract. It is the physical anchor that lets a digital monetary system remain tied to reality instead of to rhetoric or political decree.
