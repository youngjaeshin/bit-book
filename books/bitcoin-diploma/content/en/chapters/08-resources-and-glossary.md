# Resources & Glossary

## Chapter Digest

The closing materials shift from argument to continuation. The additional resources and glossary make clear that Bitcoin education does not end with one book. Instead, understanding grows through communities, media, technical exploration, and repeated return to key concepts. The glossary in particular turns scattered unfamiliar words into a navigable vocabulary, reinforcing that learning Bitcoin requires learning a new language of money and technology.

This back matter therefore extends the educational function of the book. It points the reader outward toward further study, participation, and practice. In that sense, the ending remains consistent with the book’s larger project: Bitcoin is not just something to admire from a distance, but something to keep learning, using, and integrating into one’s own life.

## Key Takeaways

- Bitcoin learning is presented as ongoing rather than complete.
- Glossaries and resources turn complexity into navigable knowledge.
- Education extends through communities, media, and practice.
- The ending directs readers outward toward continued participation.
- The book closes by reinforcing Bitcoin as a lived discipline.

## Notable Terms / Claims

- Ongoing Bitcoin education
- Vocabulary of money and technology
- Learning through community
- Resources for participation
- Education as practice
