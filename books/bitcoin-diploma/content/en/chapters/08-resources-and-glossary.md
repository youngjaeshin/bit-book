# Resources & Glossary

## Chapter Digest

The closing material shifts from argument to continuation. The additional resources and glossary make it clear that Bitcoin education does not end with one book. Understanding grows through communities, media, technical exploration, and repeated return to the core ideas. The glossary in particular gathers unfamiliar terms into a navigable vocabulary, showing that learning Bitcoin also means learning a new language of money and technology.

This back matter extends the educational purpose of the book. It points the reader outward toward further study, participation, and practice. The ending remains consistent with the book’s larger message: Bitcoin is not something to admire from a distance, but something to keep learning, using, and integrating into life.
