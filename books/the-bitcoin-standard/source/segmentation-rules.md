- Use the English original EPUB as the source of truth.
- Ignore the Korean EPUB structure for segmentation purposes.
- Convert the original chapter structure into 16 web-summary units.

- Merge Chapter 1 + Chapter 2 into one early foundations unit.
- Split Chapters 4, 5, 6, 7, and 10 using their internal section anchors.
- Merge back matter into one closing unit.
